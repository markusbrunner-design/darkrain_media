The lie

Wouldn’t it be so empty with you?
Some nights I feel sorry for some of my faults,
but it’s so hard to find the right way.

And I went into the light
and I did what I thought was right.
And now I’m here alone –
maybe I was wrong.

There have been many changes in my life.
Some of my decisions, I have made,
they were not right.
Some were – let’s say – I’m not really sure.

And I went into the light
and I did what I thought was right.
And now I’m here alone –
maybe I was wrong.

I hated the rules,
the things we were
used to, used to do in every day
I think it was it to leave and not to stay.

And I went into the light
and I did what I thought was right.
And now I’m here alone –
maybe I was wrong.
