Straßenkinderlied 2

What can I say if you don’t listen?
What can I show if you don’t watch?

I’m sick of fighting,
sick of hearing you both,
saying ugly words, worst words.
This is not a family this is hell to me.
Hating you for doing this
to someone you call your baby.

I often think about trying it again,
trying to reach your attention.
Should I give up? Should I give up
and make my own way?

See all the happiness around me
and the mess in my home, in my heart.

In the night my mind disappears,
going on a journey watching
the sun rising again in a better world.
I think I’m jealous at that beauty I’m missing.

I often think about trying it again,
trying to reach your attention.
Should I give up? Should I give up
and make my own way?

I see myself in a brighter light.
I see myself new.
I see myself strong enough
to leave you
and make my own way.
