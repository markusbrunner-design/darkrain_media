Fight

It’s a human fault,
won’t and can’t be sensible,
won’t and can’t be responsible.
I can’t see, I can’t find.
There will be another war again.

And we fight – tell me will it ever stop?
And we fight we fight.
And we fight we fight.
And please let it stop.

I don’t see myself as a victim,
I don’t think the whole world is against me.
I am a because so are you,
so are you, such as you.

And we fight – tell me will it ever stop?
And we fight we fight.
And we fight we fight.
And please let it stop.

Afterwards the most of us feel sorry,
expecting about what we’ve done wrong.
But all the "I must, I must have"
are just forming circles, circles.

And we fight – tell me will it ever stop?
And we fight we fight.
And we fight we fight.
And please let it stop.
