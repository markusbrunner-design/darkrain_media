See you again

The lightest blue doesn’t reach me.
The sun only comes to my skin.
All this makes me so weird.

And then I see you again.
And the dark don’t seem so dark anymore.
And at least for a while,
and at least as long as I see you smile.

The sky turns black in my world.
The rain to acid in my world.
My feelings are mixed,
taste so strange.

And then I see you again.
And the dark don’t seem so dark anymore.
And at least for a while,
and at least as long as I see you smile.

I wonder if, I wonder if this is me
in that moment I can see my face in your eyes.
But the tiniest piece of sense
makes me hope again.

And at least for a while,
and at least as long as I see you smile.
